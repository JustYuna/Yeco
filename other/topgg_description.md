### 🌟 What is Yeco?
**Yeco** is a cross-server economy bot designed to bring a persistent, dynamic financial system to your Discord community. Unlike local economy bots, your progress and balance follow you across every server Yeco is added to.

### 💫 Why Yeco?
* **Zero-Friction Setup:** No complex dashboards or configuration needed. Invite the bot and start your journey instantly.
* **True Global Persistence:** Your currency and levels follow you across every server Yeco is added to.
* **Completely Free-to-Play:** Access all core features without paywalls. *Note: High-capacity servers may be subject to performance-based tiers to maintain hosting stability.*
* **Frequently Maintained:** Enjoy a living economy with constant updates, seasonal events, and new features driven by community feedback via `/report`.

### 🛠️ Core Features
* **💼 Diverse Careers:** Unlock unique jobs and work your way to the top.
* **🎲 High Stakes:** Test your luck with Gambling, or take a risk with Robbing and Heists.
* **🏦 Secure Banking:** Keep your hard-earned coins safe from thieves.
* **📈 Global Persistence:** Your stats and inventory stay with you everywhere.

### 🚀 Getting Started
Setting up Yeco is easy! Use the following commands to begin your journey:
* `/help` - View the full list of commands.
* `/start` - Create your global profile.
* `/farm` - Earn your first set of coins.
